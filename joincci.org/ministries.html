Get involved

Celebration Church Ministries are expressions of our mandate as a Church and as a people. We serve God by his spirit- Joyfully.

Celebration Church Worship

Celebration Church Worship is the music ministry of Celebration Church International, dedicated to creating inspiring music with a strong foundation in faith. Led by our founder, Apostle Emmanuel Iren, our mission is to produce songs that deepen individuals' relationship with Christ and spread a message of hope and joy through music

Finding Salaam

Finding Salaam is an evangelical ministry of Celebration Church International, led by Apostle Emmanuel Iren. Our mission is to share the Gospel with the Muslim community and guide individuals toward peace, purpose, and a deeper understanding of our faith, while offering support through life's challenges.

Celeb Kids & Teens

Celeb Kids is the children's ministry of Celebration Church International. Our vision is to see all children celebrating endless life in Christ and training them to disciple others for Christ. Led by our founder, Emmanuel Iren, we are committed to partnering with you and your child for their progress and joy in the faith
