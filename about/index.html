about us

We live purpose-driven, results-oriented lives rooted in God's Word

MorningStar Christian Centre is a vibrant Word-based, Christ-centred, Spirit-filled, Spirit-led Church where Jesus is unarguably Lord. Our passion is soul-winning and soul-cultivation, and we do this in a spiritually conducive atmosphere of real fellowship with the brethren.

As a Word-based ministry, we’re focused on raising a people full of the Word, yielded to the Spirit and committed to God’s purpose for our lives. We believe and engage the power of discipleship to raise and train men who will be mightily used of God, just as our Lord Jesus did during His life here on earth. (Mark 3:13-15)

We have been called to host the revival for this region and beyond. The Lord said to us, “from this place will sound forth the Word throughout this region!

what we Believe

Our Christian principles

The Bible

The Bible is God’s word to us to lead us to Himself and to guide our lives. It is without error and is the authority on which we base our faith, conduct and teachings.

The Trinity

The Bible is God’s word to us to lead us to Himself and to guide our lives. It is without error and is the authority on which we base our faith, conduct and teachings.

The Cross

Jesus died on the cross, shed His blood for our sins and rose again on the third day. Salvation is found by placing our faith in what Jesus did for us on the cross.

Salvation

Salvation is a total package which includes your health, wealth, holiness, marital bliss and that salvation is not an end, but the means to an end.

Our Inheritance

As children of God, we are overcomers, and more than conquerors and God intends for each of us to experience the abundant life He has in store for us.

Holy-Spirit Baptism

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit evidenced by the speaking of other tongues.

Water Baptism

We believe in the baptism of water by immersion signifying the death, burial and resurrection of our Lord Jesus.

Prayer

We believe in the power and efficacy of prayer.

Holy Commnunion

We believe in the Holy Communion signifying the body and blood of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Soul Winning/Cultivation

Soul winning our urgent task. Soul cultivation establishing the saints.

The Second Coming

We believe in the second coming of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.

Heaven and Hell

Heaven and hell are real places. Death is a beginning, not the end.
