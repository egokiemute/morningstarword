about us

We live purpose-driven, results-oriented lives rooted in God's Word

MorningStar Christian Centre is a vibrant Word-based, Christ-centred, Spirit-filled and Spirit-led Church where Jesus is unarguably Lord. Our passion is soul-winning and cultivation, and we do this in a spiritually conducive atmosphere of real fellowship with believers.

As a Word-based ministry, we're focused on raising a people full of the Word, yielded to the Spirit and committed to God’s purpose for our lives. We believe and engage the power of discipleship to raise and train men who will be mightily used by God, just as our Lord Jesus did during His life here on earth. (Mark 3:13-15)

We have been called to host the revival for this region and beyond. The Lord said to us, “from this place will sound forth the Word throughout this region!

what we Believe

Our Christian principles

The Bible

The Bible is God’s word to us to lead us to Himself and to guide our lives. It is without error and is the authority on which we base our faith, conduct and teachings.

The Trinity

We believe that God exists as three distinct persons—Father, Son (Jesus), and Holy Spirit—in one essence. They are co-equal, co-eternal, and work in perfect unity. This truth is central to our understanding of God's nature and guides our faith and life.

The Cross

Jesus died on the cross, shed His blood for our sins and rose again on the third day. Salvation is found by placing our faith in what Jesus did for us on the cross.

Salvation

Salvation is a total package which includes your health, wealth, holiness, marital bliss and that salvation is not an end, but the means to an end.

Our Inheritance

As children of God, we are overcomers, and more than conquerors, and God intends for each of us to experience the abundant life He has in store for us.

Holy-Spirit Baptism

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit evidenced by the speaking of other tongues.

Water Baptism

We believe in the baptism of water by immersion signifying the death, burial and resurrection of our Lord Jesus.

Prayer

We believe in the power and efficacy of prayer.

Holy Commnunion

We believe in the Holy Communion signifying the body and blood of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Soul Winning/Cultivation

Soul winning, our urgent task. Soul cultivation, establishing the saints.

The Second Coming

We believe in the second coming of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.

Heaven and Hell

Heaven and hell are real places. Death is a beginning, not the end.

our core value

Empowering Lives through The Word and Spirit.

'Empowerment' is a core value because it encapsulates the church's mission to equip individuals with God’s Word, nurture spiritual growth, and enable them to live Spirit-led, purpose-driven lives. It reflects the commitment to discipleship, soul-winning, and building a community where believers are strengthened to fulfill God’s plan and advance His kingdom.

The church demonstrates love by emphasizing salvation as a total package that encompasses health, wealth, holiness, and marital bliss, ensuring that every believer experiences the abundant life God has in store. It engages in soul-winning and soul-cultivation, providing a spiritually conducive atmosphere where people can experience real fellowship and transformation.

Community is fostered through shared practices like Holy Communion, prayer, and fellowship, which bring members together in unity. By cultivating a spiritually enriching environment focused on discipleship and the Word, the church ensures every member feels included in God’s supernatural army and connected to His purpose.

Spiritual growth is vital for living a Spirit-filled, purpose-driven life rooted in God’s Word. It enables believers to be empowered, trained, and transformed into individuals mightily used by God, helping them take their place in His agenda and preparing for eternal life.

These values shape the church’s activities by driving soul-winning efforts and encouraging discipleship through Bible-based teachings and practices. Events and meetings are designed to cultivate spiritual growth, build community, and advance God’s kingdom, ensuring all members align with His purpose.

A vibrant, Christ-centered, Spirit-filled church where Jesus reigns as Lord. Passionate about winning and nurturing souls, we create a spiritually rich atmosphere for true fellowship.
