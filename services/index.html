our core value

Empowering Lives through The Word and Spirit.

'Empowerment' is a core value because it encapsulates the church's mission to equip individuals with God’s Word, nurture spiritual growth, and enable them to live Spirit-led, purpose-driven lives. It reflects the commitment to discipleship, soul-winning, and building a community where believers are strengthened to fulfill God’s plan and advance His kingdom.

The church demonstrates love by emphasizing salvation as a total package that encompasses health, wealth, holiness, and marital bliss, ensuring that every believer experiences the abundant life God has in store. It engages in soul-winning and soul-cultivation, providing a spiritually conducive atmosphere where people can experience real fellowship and transformation.

Community is fostered through shared practices like Holy Communion, prayer, and fellowship, which bring members together in unity. By cultivating a spiritually enriching environment focused on discipleship and the Word, the church ensures every member feels included in God’s supernatural army and connected to His purpose.

Spiritual growth is vital for living a Spirit-filled, purpose-driven life rooted in God’s Word. It enables believers to be empowered, trained, and transformed into individuals mightily used by God, helping them take their place in His agenda and preparing for eternal life.

These values shape the church’s activities by driving soul-winning efforts and encouraging discipleship through Bible-based teachings and practices. Events and meetings are designed to cultivate spiritual growth, build community, and advance God’s kingdom, ensuring all members align with His purpose.

A vibrant, Christ-centered, Spirit-filled church where Jesus reigns as Lord. Passionate about winning and nurturing souls, we create a spiritually rich atmosphere for true fellowship.
